Conference Information

Assessing Public Awareness

What are the most effective ways to capture the public’s understanding of mosquito borne diseases? How to use these findings to inform the design of campaigns to improve public awareness?

Date

August 31, 2016

Time

10am - 12pm EST

Participate
Problem Description

Each online session is focused on a specific problem, that has been fleshed out into a briefing to describe the root causes of the problem, current approaches in different countries, and lessons learned from other domains.

Read the Problem Description
Apply to Participate

Would you like to lend your skills to tackle a public health emergency? Deliberate with diverse innovators and professionals to refine good ideas into actionable projects? Join a conversation with government partners interested in implementing new approaches to combatting Zika and other mosquito-borne diseases.
